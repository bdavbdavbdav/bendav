DAY (2014-15)

for percussion solo, duo, trio, and piano


watch on youtube

performance by Clocks in Motion


program note:

DAY divides the stage into four groups with four different kinds of instrumentation and music: Dawn, Noon, Dusk, and Midnight. Each group perform a number of mostly miniature movements that create larger trajectories. These trajectories at times merge together and at other times stand completely independent to the other ensembles.
